About Orion GLE

As a family-operated business, we work with speed, flexibility, and clear intent. This allows us to adapt quickly, collaborate closely, and tailor every solution to your goals and technical requirements. We believe strong digital products are built on trusted partnerships, transparent communication, and disciplined execution.

At Orion GLE, we do more than build apps and websites. We turn ideas into reliable, future-ready platforms through clean architecture, thoughtful design, and practical innovation. Your trust drives our commitment to quality, accountability, and ongoing support at every stage.

What We Do

  • Website development for businesses, products, and service platforms.
  • Application development for iOS and web-based products.
  • Modernisation of existing apps and websites, including UX, speed, and reliability.
  • Post-launch support, iterative improvements, and release management.

How We Do It

  • We define outcomes first: business goals, users, scope, and commercial priorities.
  • We design for clarity through structured journeys, wireframes, and conversion-focused UX.
  • We build in short sprints with visible progress and regular review points.
  • We test thoroughly before launch, then support performance and iteration.

How an App or Website Is Built

Our delivery framework is structured, transparent, and designed to reduce risk while maintaining momentum.

1. Discovery

We define business goals, user needs, required features, and technical constraints.

2. Architecture

We map system structure, data models, integrations, and delivery milestones.

3. UX & UI Design

We craft layouts and interactions that are clear, modern, and conversion-focused.

4. Development

We build core functionality in agile cycles with consistent progress reviews.

5. QA & Optimisation

We test usability, stability, speed, and security before release.

6. Launch & Support

We deploy, monitor, and continue improving performance after go-live.
