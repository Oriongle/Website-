Cookie Policy

Effective date: February 17, 2026

This policy explains how ORION GLE LTD uses cookies and similar technologies on our website and portal.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. We also use local storage and similar technologies to remember preferences and improve service delivery.

2. Cookie categories we use

  • Strictly necessary: required for security, login sessions, and core functionality.
  • Analytics (optional): help us understand usage and improve performance, only where consented.

3. Cookies and local storage keys in use

  • orion_portal_session (cookie): maintains authenticated portal sessions (essential).
  • orion_cookie_consent_v1 (local storage): stores your cookie preference choice (essential for consent management).
  • _ga / _ga_* (Google Analytics, where enabled): analytics identifiers used only after optional consent.

4. Legal basis

We use essential cookies based on legitimate interests/strict necessity for requested services. We use optional analytics cookies on the basis of your consent, where required by law.

5. Managing cookie choices

  • Use our consent banner to accept or reject optional analytics cookies.
  • You can block/delete cookies in your browser settings at any time.
  • If you clear browser storage, the consent banner may be shown again.

6. Third-party cookies

Where analytics are enabled and consent is given, third-party services (for example Google Analytics) may set cookies in line with their own policies.

7. Contact

For cookie questions, contact support@oriongle.co.uk.
