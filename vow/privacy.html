Orion GLE Ltd is the data controller for VOW Focus.

We may process:

We process data to:

4. Legal Bases (UK GDPR)

Depending on context, we rely on:

We do not sell personal data.

We may share limited data with service providers / processors needed to operate VOW Focus (for example Apple for in-app purchases).

6. International Transfers

If data is processed outside the UK, we use lawful transfer mechanisms and safeguards where required.

7. Retention

Typical retention periods:

  • App progress / settings: while account or app use is active; removed on valid deletion request (unless legally required otherwise)

  • Support records: up to 24 months from last interaction

You may have rights to:

  • Request deletion

To make a request, email support@oriongle.co.uk.

Subject line suggestion: “GDPR Request”

Where direct marketing is used, we provide opt-in / opt-out controls and unsubscribe options.

We maintain incident response procedures and notify regulators or users where legally required.
